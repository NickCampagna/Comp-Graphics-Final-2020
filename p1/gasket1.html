Instructions: Select a shape and/or transformation above, fill out whatever information is required below.
"Display Shape" creates a shape of your selection with default coordinates, BUT if you'd rather draw it, click "Enable Shape Drawing"
To draw your shape, click on the canvas. With most shapes, you only need to click two (or three, if drawing a triangle) times.
If you're drawing a poly-line or polygon, you can click an infinite number of times, then finish by right-clicking to mark the final point.
Shape ID is for selecting the shape you'd like to transform by it's numerical ID. The first shape drawn has an ID of 0, the next one's is 1, etc...
dX and dY are for describing "how much" the shape should be transformed by. (dX: 20, dY: 20 on Translate moves the shape 20px right and down).
(only dX is used for Rotate)
The R, G, B fields are for selecting color for the next shape drawn.
